About FSCM

Promoting figure skating in Madison since 1963.

Our History

Our Story

The Figure Skating Club of Madison was founded on December 2, 1963 as a nonprofit dedicated to promoting figure skating in the greater Madison area.

For over 60 years, FSCM has provided a supportive community for skaters of all ages and skill levels. We are a proud affiliated member club of U.S. Figure Skating.

Today, our club has more than 180 members and contracts over 500 annual ice hours at two home arenas.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

FSCM exists to promote and advance figure skating through:

  • Providing quality ice time and training facilities
  • Supporting skaters in competition and testing
  • Producing annual ice shows and community events
  • Maintaining a welcoming skating community
  • Encouraging sportsmanship and personal growth
