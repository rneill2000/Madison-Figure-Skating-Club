What We Ask

Requirements

To join FSCM, skaters should meet these criteria:

  • At least one family member must be 18+
  • Skaters must have achieved Basic 2 level or higher
  • Annual $100 facilities maintenance donation (2025–2026)
  • Most memberships require 15 volunteer hours per year
What You Get

Member Benefits

Being part of FSCM gives you access to:

  • Lowest ice rates at two home arenas
  • USFS-sanctioned competition eligibility
  • Discounted test session fees
  • Annual ice show participation
  • Access to 15 certified coaches
  • Community events and social activities
  • Skate sharpening services
