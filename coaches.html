Guidance

Finding the Right Coach

Choosing a coach is an important decision. Consider your skating goals, preferred training style, and schedule.

Our coaches utilize on-ice and off-ice training methods, including harness work and contemporary techniques.

U.S. Figure Skating offers resources to help you select the right coach.

Standards

Coach Requirements

All FSCM coaches must maintain:

  • U.S. Figure Skating membership
  • Core Certified Coach status
  • Coach I.C.E. certification
  • Current registration with FSCM
  • Continuing education compliance
